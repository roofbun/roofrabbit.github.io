Nightmare

Skills

  • Video Editing

Programs

  • Premiere Pro

Description

A class assignment to edit a three stage nightmare sequence with provided footage.

This was the final for my Level I Video & Sound Production class. We were required to include three story points: the nightmare, our protaganist waking up from it, and the retelling of it to her roommate. There was a lot of good shots of the villain in the yellow suit, so I chose to integrate them into flashbacks during the retelling scene.
