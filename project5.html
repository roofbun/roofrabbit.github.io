Description

An assignment to illustrate 10 items using Photoshop's brush tools.

This was one of my favorite projects in Photoshop Raster Graphics. For the assignment option I chose, we needed to draw 10 different versions of one thing, utilizing Photoshop's layers, brushes, and adjustment layers. If you need to draw 10 of something, why not cats? I wanted every cat to have a different personality, action, and coloration.
